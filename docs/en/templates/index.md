# Templates

Practical templates for AI Act compliance at BAUER GROUP. All templates are designed as starting points and should be adapted to the specific use case.

| Template | Purpose | AI Act Reference |
|----------|---------|-----------------|
| [AI System Inventory](/en/templates/ki-inventar) | Recording all AI systems in the organisation | Art. 4, Art. 49 |
| [Risk Classification Form](/en/templates/risikoklassifizierung) | Classification into the four risk levels | Art. 6 |
| [Non-High-Risk Assessment](/en/templates/nicht-hochrisiko) | Documentation of non-high-risk classification | Art. 6(3)/(4) |
| [EU Declaration of Conformity](/en/templates/konformitaetserklaerung) | Legally binding declaration of conformity | Art. 47, Annex V |
| [Transparency Notice](/en/templates/transparenz-hinweis) | User notice for AI interaction | Art. 50 |
| [AI Literacy Training Record](/en/templates/ai-literacy) | Documentation of training participation | Art. 4 |
| [Go/No-Go Decision Protocol](/en/templates/go-no-go) | Market entry decision | Internal |
