# France

France has **not yet conclusively** designated its competent authorities. The Directorate-General for Enterprise (**DGE**) published a draft designation of market surveillance authorities in **September 2025**.

A **decentralised** model with up to **17 market surveillance authorities** is envisaged. Three authorities are expected to carry the main load:

- **CNIL** (data protection authority) — lead for AI involving personal data
- **DGCCRF** (consumer protection / competition / fraud control)
- **ARCOM** (audiovisual and digital regulator)

::: warning Status
Formal designation is still pending (as of June 2026). The CNIL already acts in practice as the central point of contact for AI matters involving personal data.
:::
