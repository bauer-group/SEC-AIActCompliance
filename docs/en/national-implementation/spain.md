# Spain – AESIA

Spain was the **first** EU member state to create a dedicated AI supervisory authority: **AESIA** (Agencia Española de Supervisión de la Inteligencia Artificial), established by Royal Decree 729/2023.

- **operational since June 2024**, headquartered in A Coruña
- **full sanctioning powers since August 2025**
- coordinates with the data protection authority **AEPD**, the **Banco de España** and the securities regulator **CNMV**

A national AI bill ("Anteproyecto de Ley para el buen uso y la gobernanza de la Inteligencia Artificial") was presented in **March 2025** and complements the institutional requirements. AESIA has also published several practical compliance guides.
