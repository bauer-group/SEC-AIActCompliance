# Ireland

Ireland follows a **decentralised** oversight model: instead of a single authority, **15 competent authorities** have been designated, each responsible for its sector.

- coordinated by a **National AI Office**, operational since **September 2025**
- the single point of contact and cross-sector coordination run through this office

The decentralised model leverages existing sector regulators; for companies this means that different authorities may be competent depending on the area of application.

::: tip BAUER GROUP Relevance
When placing products on the Irish market, the competent authority depends on the application; the National AI Office serves as the central point of contact.
:::
