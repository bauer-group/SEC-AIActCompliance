# Austria

Austria has set up an **AI Service Office** (KI-Servicestelle) at the **RTR** (Rundfunk und Telekom Regulierungs-GmbH), serving as a national competence and information centre for AI (legal basis includes KommAustria-Gesetz §20c, TKG 2021 §194a).

- The AI Service Office supports authorities, businesses and civil society in implementing the AI Act.
- The government plans to **expand it into a full market surveillance authority** with extended powers.
- At least one **AI regulatory sandbox** must be established by 2 August 2026 (Art. 57).

::: warning Status
The final authority architecture (full market surveillance) is still being built (as of June 2026).
:::

::: tip BAUER GROUP Relevance
When placing AI systems on the Austrian market, the RTR's AI Service Office is the central point of contact; the full market surveillance authority is currently being established.
:::
