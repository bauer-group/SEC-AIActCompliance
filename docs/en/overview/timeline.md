# Timeline & Deadlines

## Phased Applicability

The AI Act becomes applicable in stages. The following deadlines are operationally relevant for the BAUER GROUP:

| Date | Obligation | Status | BAUER GROUP Relevance |
|---|---|---|---|
| **01.08.2024** | Entry into force | ✅ Completed | Preparation phase |
| **02.02.2025** | Prohibited AI practices (Art. 5) + AI Literacy (Art. 4) | ✅ Applicable | Review of all products against the prohibition catalogue |
| **02.08.2025** | GPAI obligations (Art. 51–56), governance infrastructure, sanctions regime | ✅ Applicable | Relevant only as GPAI deployer |
| **02.08.2026** | Transparency obligations (Art. 50) | ⏳ Deadline | Disclosure duties for chatbots and AI-generated content |
| **02.12.2026** | Marking of synthetic content (Art. 50(2)) + new prohibition NCII/CSAM (Art. 5) | ⏳ Transitional deadline | Machine-readable labelling of AI-generated outputs |
| **02.12.2027** | High-risk AI systems Annex III (stand-alone, Art. 6(2)) | ⏳ Deadline (Omnibus) | **Main deadline** – all high-risk systems must be compliant |
| **02.08.2028** | High-risk AI as safety components in regulated products (Annex I) | ⏳ Deadline (Omnibus) | Embedded AI in machinery, medical devices etc. |

::: warning Digital Omnibus – Postponed High-Risk Deadlines (as of June 2026)
The European Commission's original "Digital Omnibus" proposal (19 Nov 2025) became a **provisional agreement** between Parliament and Council on **7 May 2026**. It postpones the high-risk obligations to **fixed** dates (without the originally proposed standards trigger):

- Annex III (stand-alone high-risk systems): **02.08.2026 → 02.12.2027**
- Annex I (embedded in regulated products): **02.08.2027 → 02.08.2028**

**Not postponed:** GPAI obligations, AI literacy, the existing prohibitions (Art. 5) and the transparency obligations under Art. 50 (still 02.08.2026); only the marking of synthetic content under Art. 50(2) receives a grace period until 02.12.2026.

**Legal status:** The agreement is **not yet** published in the EU Official Journal. Until formal adoption, the dates 02.08.2026 and 02.08.2027 formally remain in force. BAUER GROUP plans along the postponed dates, keeps **02.08.2026 as the legal fallback** in view and tracks the formal adoption.
:::

## Operative Timeline BAUER GROUP

### Already Completed (Q1 2025)

- AI Literacy: foundational training for all employees with AI exposure
- Review of the product portfolio against the prohibition catalogue (Art. 5)
- Creation of AI system inventory (draft)

### Ongoing (2025–2026)

- Risk classification of all identified AI systems
- Go/No-Go assessment for high-risk candidates
- Development of transparency notices for limited-risk systems
- Documentation of this compliance site

### Deadline 02.08.2026 – Transparency

- Disclosure duties for AI interaction (chatbots) implemented
- Machine-readable labelling of AI-generated content prepared (mandatory from 02.12.2026)

### Deadline 02.12.2027 – High-Risk (Annex III)

- Conformity assessment for all high-risk systems (where a Go decision has been taken)
- EU database registration
- CE marking
- QMS evidence
